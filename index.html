About the Stylist

Samir

Samir is picky, passionate, and takes his time as a stylist who cares more about how something is done than how fast it’s finished.

He’s focused on the simple things: clean work, honest advice, and hair that grows out properly.

Every appointment gets his full attention. Whether it’s a quick cleanup or a full color correction, he’s fully in it until it’s right.

You’ll usually catch him mid-foils, with a bite of tabbouleh nearby, and a football match on in the background.

Clients usually describe him the same way: someone who remembers your hair, your patterns, and how you like to feel when you leave the chair.
