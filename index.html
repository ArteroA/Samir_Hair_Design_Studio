About the Stylist

Samir

Samir is a strongly opinionated, old-school craftsman — the kind of stylist who believes precision matters more than speed, and honesty matters more than trends.

Every appointment receives his full attention. He puts 100% into every cut, color, and correction — whether it’s simple maintenance or full transformation.

On any given day, you might find him mid-foils taking a quiet bite of tabbouleh, or glancing at a football match before returning to his work. Nothing rushed. Nothing half-finished.

Despite his direct nature, clients often describe something consistent in him: a familiar presence. Someone who remembers your hair, your patterns, and the way you like to feel when you leave the chair.
